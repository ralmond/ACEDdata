International time is based on the time in Greenwich, England, which is in time zone 0. There are twelve time zones to the west and twelve times zones to the east of time zone 0. As one moves to the east of time zone 0, 1 hour is added for each time zone. As one moves to the west of time zone 0, 1 hour is subtracted for each time zone. New York is 5 times zones to the west of Greenwich, and Finland is 2 time zones to the east of Greenwich.
