Dominoes are rectangular-shaped tiles. Each tile is divided by a line across the center that separates the piece into two ends. Each end may have between 0 and 6 dots (an end with 0 dots appears blank). You can arrange dominoes in a chain by matching the number of dots on the ends where they touch.

Stephanie has a set of dominoes. She removes all of the dominoes that have an end with no dots (blanks) on them. She selects four dominoes from the remaining pile and arranges them end to end, as shown in the figure below.
